VikiClow is an execution system you run on your own devices.

Pair this iPhone app with your VikiClow Gateway to turn your phone into a secure node for voice, camera, screen, location, and device-aware workflows.

What you can do:
- Launch and monitor missions from iPhone
- Use voice wake and push-to-talk
- Capture photos and short clips on request
- Record screen snippets for troubleshooting and workflows
- Share text, links, and media directly into VikiClow
- Run location-aware and device-aware automations

VikiClow is local-first: you control your gateway, keys, memory, and configuration.

Getting started:
1) Set up your VikiClow Gateway
2) Open the iOS app and pair with your gateway
3) Start running commands, missions, and automations from your phone
